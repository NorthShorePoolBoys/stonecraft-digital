CHIMNEY SERVICES

New England's harsh winters take a brutal toll on chimneys. Freeze-thaw cycles crack mortar joints, deteriorate crowns, and compromise flashing. New England Pavers & Stone provides expert chimney repair, repointing, and full chimney rebuilds for homeowners across Wakefield and Greater Boston.

TUCKPOINTING & REPOINTING

We carefully remove deteriorated mortar and replace it with fresh, color-matched mortar that restores structural integrity and visual appearance.

CROWN REPAIR & REPLACEMENT

Cracked chimney crowns allow water infiltration that accelerates deterioration. We repair or rebuild crowns with proper slope and drip edges.

FULL CHIMNEY REBUILDS

When damage extends beyond repair, we dismantle and rebuild chimneys from the roofline up using matching brick and proper flashing integration.

FLASHING & WATERPROOFING

We install step flashing, counter-flashing, and cricket diverters to prevent the water penetration that causes most chimney failures.

We offer free chimney inspections and estimates. Our masons will assess the condition of your chimney and recommend the most cost-effective repair strategy.
